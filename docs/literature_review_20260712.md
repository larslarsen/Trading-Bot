# Crypto Algo Trading Literature Review
Date: 2026-07-12

## Helformer Cluster
- Main paper: Kehinde et al., 2025, Journal of Big Data
- Nature: attention-based transformer for next-day crypto price forecasting
- Citations found: 45+ papers mostly citing as benchmark
- Assessment: forecasting/price-prediction papers, NOT trading systems. They predict direction/level but not entry/exit/sizing.

## Broader Literature

### 1. Volatility-Adaptive Trend Following
- URL: https://papers.ssrn.com/sol3/papers.cfm?abstract_id=5821842
- Author: I Karassavidis, 2025
- Description: trend-following framework that adjusts to volatility in high-frequency cryptocurrency markets, focus on BTC
- Relevance: HIGH - directly tests volatility-adaptive trend on crypto

### 2. Systematic Mapping Study
- URL: https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S2667096824000296
- Author: DTA Nguyen, 2024, Cited by 21
- Description: systematic mapping approach examining current state of cryptocurrency trading research
- Relevance: HIGH - comprehensive overview of what works/doesn't
